Cost of Living Index by Country 2016

Indices Explanation



Ask A Question

How can we quickly check cost of living indix of a country or an area in the world? What is a rough distribution of countries and areas with low/high cost of living index?

Answer It With Data

We marked 122 countries and areas in the world on a map with dots in different colors (the first 40 or so countries/areas with highest cost of living index are in red; the 40 or so in the middle are in purple; the last 40 or so are in yellow) as shown above. To find cost of living indices of a country or an area in 2016, we can locate it on the map and check out its indices by clicking the corresponding dot.

From the map, we can see that in 2016 countries and areas with high cost of living index are mainly in Europe, Middle East, North and Central America, and East Asia. Most of them are coastal countries and areas. Countries and areas with low cost of living index are mainly in Africa, Central and South Asia, and South America. Most of them are landlocked countries and areas.
